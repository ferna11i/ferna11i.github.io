Computer Vision

This field encomposses all types of tasks ranging from classifying the entire image into one of multiple classes to detecting a certain object in an image. With the availability of libraries such as Pytorch developing Deep Learning solutions to solve such task has become more efficient. Here are a the projects that I am currently working on to learn and implement Deep Learning based solutions

Face Mask Detection

With the current COVID crisis that dominates the world we must ensure to protect others as well as ourselfs by wearing face masks appropriately whenever we step into an enclosed area. This is the second line of defence after receiving two doses of vaccinations.

This project deals with identifying if people in images have worn their face masks or not. It also checks to see if the person is wearing the face mask incorrectly.
